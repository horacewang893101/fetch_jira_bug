# MP-401: [Recommendation] Cost Management - Enable anomaly detection

**Status:** Backlog

**Assignee:** Unassigned

**Description:**
No anomaly detection is enabled for either RG during initial installation.

*Impact:*
Unexpected spikes may go unnoticed until late in the billing cycle.

*Recommended Fix:*

* Enable Cost Anomaly Detection for both RGs and route notifications to owners and default notification channels.
