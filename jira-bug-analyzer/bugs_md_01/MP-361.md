# MP-361: Missing Labels for Form Fields

**Status:** Backlog

**Assignee:** Unassigned

**Description:**
*Priority*: High  
*Impact*: Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA - 1.3.1, 3.3.2), Screen Reader Support, Form Usability  
*Category*: Labels and Instructions

*Problem*:

* Form fields (e.g., Username, Password on the login page) do not have visible {{<label>}} elements
* Placeholders are used as the only identification and disappear after typing
* Screen readers may not correctly identify each field’s purpose
* Users with cognitive difficulties may forget which field they are filling in

*Affected Elements*:

* Login page: "Username" and "Password" fields
* Create Agent, MCP, Sensitive Words forms
* Search fields across the application
* Many other locations

*WCAG Reference*:

* 1.3.1 Info and Relationships – labels must be programmatically associated with fields
* 3.3.2 Labels or Instructions – fields require labels or instructions

*Recommended Fix*:

{code:html}<!-- Instead of: -->
<input type="text" placeholder="Username" />

<!-- Use: -->
<label for="username">Username</label>
<input type="text" id="username" placeholder="Enter your username" />

<!-- Or provide a visually-hidden label for screen readers: -->
<label for="username" class="sr-only">Username</label>
<input type="text" id="username" placeholder="Username" aria-label="Username" />{code}
