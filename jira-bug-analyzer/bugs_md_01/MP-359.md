# MP-359: Missing or Inadequate Alternative Text (alt) for Images Across the Entire Application

**Status:** Backlog

**Assignee:** Unassigned

**Description:**
*Priority*: Critical  
*Impact*: Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 Level A - 1.1.1), Text Alternatives, Screen Reader Support  
*Category*: Non-text Content

*Problem*:
Most images in the application do not have correct alternative text (the {{alt}} attribute). Images are either missing {{alt}} entirely or have generic/non-descriptive values. This violates *WCAG Level A* (a foundational requirement), which means the content becomes inaccessible for blind users.

*Affected Elements*:

* *Agent avatars*: generic alt values (e.g., "Agent", "Alice") instead of describing the agent’s purpose
* *Feature icons*: Deep Research, AI Search, AI Translator cards – images with missing or empty alt
* *Application logo*: may not have an appropriate alt text
* *Card images*: agent illustrations, category icons
* *Decorative images*: not marked with {{alt=""}} or {{role="presentation"}}
* *Empty-state illustrations*: e.g., "No Data" without contextual alt
* *Navigation icons*: small images without text alternatives
* *Many others*: ...

*WCAG Reference*:

* 1.1.1 Non-text Content (Level A) – all images must have a text alternative describing their purpose or content

*Recommended Fix*:
Audit all images and add appropriate alt attributes:

{code:html}<!-- Agent avatars - describe purpose, not appearance -->
<img src="robot.png" alt="Quality Inspection Assistant - AI agent for manufacturing quality control">

<!-- Feature icons -->
<img src="deep-research.svg" alt="Deep Research - comprehensive AI-powered research tool">

<!-- Logo -->
<img src="logo.png" alt="AI Central - SoftwareOne">

<!-- Decorative images -->
<img src="decoration.png" alt="" role="presentation">

<!-- Empty states -->
<img src="no-data.svg" alt="No data available">

<!-- Navigation icons (if functional) -->
<img src="home-icon.svg" alt="Home">
<!-- Or if text is next to the icon -->
<img src="home-icon.svg" alt="" aria-hidden="true"> Home{code}

*Best Practices*:

# For informative images: describe the content or function
# For decorative images: use {{alt=""}}
# For linked images: describe the link destination, not the image
# Avoid words like "image", "icon", "photo" in alt text
# Keep alt text concise (ideally up to ~125 characters)
